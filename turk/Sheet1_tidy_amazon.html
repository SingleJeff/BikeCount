Instructions:

  • Please enter all fields.
  • The "Count", "Female", "Wearing Helmet", "Wrong Way Riding", and "Riding on Sidewalk" fields will have tally marks in them on most of the sheets. Please count the marks and enter the number. For example, |||| would be entered as 4.
  • Sometimes people put only numbers and sometimes they put both tallies and numbers. Eg, they'll write both |||| and 4. In that case, just enter 4.
  • If fields have no tallies or writing in them, leave them blank on the form.
  • If a mark is erased or scratched out, don't count it.
Thanks for your help with this project!
